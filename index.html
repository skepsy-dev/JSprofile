Jason Schepisi
Executive Producer


Australian Executive Producer Jason Schepisi started his career in Los Angeles working with production company giant Propaganda Films, developing his production skills under the helm of directors Dominic Sena, Michael Bay, Mark Romanek and David Fincher.

Returning to Sydney five years later, Jason opened up shop at Fox Studios and created Baby Lemonade Inc which went onto become the largest full service production company in Australia, bringing in runaway tvc production from across the globe.

Jason executive produced a slate of productions for international companies Original, Satellite, Anonymous, Epoch, Streetlight, MJZ, Fuel, Backyard, Studio UK, Flying Tiger, Gartner and Palomar…. promoting Australia as the destination of choice for crew, locations and talent and expanding the landscape for our local industry.

He then pursued his passion for architecture, designing and building his own Richard Neutra inspired houses in the hinterland of Byron Bay that have since become iconic residences.

Jason has now returned to producing including filming the David Beckham campaign for the Sands Hotel Group, working with Netflix to develop Australian filmed content and providing Producing Services to local and international production companies.

Jason Schepisi has an innate understanding of the role of a Producer, harmonising the cross-section of supporting the Director’s creative vision, fulfilling expectations of the Clients and managing the services, budget and prestige of the Production Company.

“Each project will be enjoyable to execute and successful in achievement.”
